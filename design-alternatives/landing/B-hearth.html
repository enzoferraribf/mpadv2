How it works

You open a URL. That's it. There's no sign-up, no onboarding, no permissions dialog. The pad is yours the moment you arrive.

Write in markdown and watch it render beside you. Sketch on the shared drawing surface. Drop in files that anyone in the room can grab.

Your text and drawings persist — come back tomorrow and everything is still there. Files live only as long as the session, keeping things clean.

Every pad lives at a path. Paths create trees. Trees create structure. No folders to manage — just natural hierarchy from the URL.
